# Code of Conduct

Titimangsa follows a simple contributor standard:

- Be respectful.
- Keep discussion focused on the project.
- Prefer verifiable sources and clear reasoning.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, and discriminatory language.
- Assume good intent, but correct inaccurate data or claims with evidence.

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or pull requests that make collaboration unsafe or unproductive.
